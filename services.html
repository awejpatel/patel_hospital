Our Story

Excellence in Healthcare Since 1985

Founded in 1985, Patel Hospitals has grown from a small community clinic to one of the region's most trusted multi-specialty hospitals. Our commitment to patient-centric care has never wavered over these four decades.

We combine the warmth of personalized care with the power of modern medicine, ensuring every patient receives not just treatment, but hope, dignity, and healing.

  • 2015 Certified
  • State-of-the-art diagnostic & surgical facilities
  • Round-the-clock emergency & critical care
  • Cashless treatment with all major insurers
Our Specialties

World-Class Medical Services

From routine check-ups to complex surgeries, our team of specialists is here for every stage of your health journey.

Cardiology

Advanced cardiac care including angioplasty, bypass surgery, and comprehensive heart health monitoring by expert cardiologists.

Paediatrics

Dedicated child health services with specialist paediatricians ensuring the best care from infancy through adolescence.

General Surgery

Minimally invasive and open surgical procedures performed by highly experienced surgeons in state-of-the-art theatres.

Ophthalmology

Complete eye care from routine examinations to advanced cataract and retinal procedures using latest technology.

Gynaecology

Comprehensive women's health services including maternity care, laparoscopic surgeries, and fertility treatments.

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency and trauma services with a fully equipped ER, rapid response teams, and critical care specialists on call.
